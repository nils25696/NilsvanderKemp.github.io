About me

I have a great affinity for numbers and a passion for working with data. I approach tasks with meticulous care and am a fast learner. Alongside theoretical knowledge acquired during my Master's degree in Business Analytics, I have also gained valuable practical experience in the world of data analysis through internships in Data Analytics and Data Science. I am particularly proficient in working with SQL and Python, as well as Power BI and Tableau for data visualization purposes. However, I am willing to become familiar with other applications and expand my repertoire.

Two of my major data projects are already highlighted below. Further projects on the topics of regression, classification and much more can be found on my GitHub.
